How to Play Wrassling

In Wrassling, the goal is simple yet wildly entertaining: toss as many opponents out of the ring as possible. Using simple two-button controls, players swing their wrestler’s arms and maneuver around the ring, battling a constant wave of challengers. The unpredictable physics make every match feel fresh, funny, and challenging.

Key Features:

  • Hilarious Physics: The unpredictable physics create comical moves and unexpected outcomes, keeping the gameplay lighthearted and fun.
  • Retro Graphics: The pixel-art style and old-school wrestling vibe appeal to both casual players and retro game lovers.
  • Endless Action: Face off against endless waves of opponents in chaotic, free-for-all matches.
  • Two-Player Mode: Play with friends in local multiplayer mode for double the fun and laughter.
